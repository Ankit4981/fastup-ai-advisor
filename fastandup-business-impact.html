Summary

Before vs After

Side-by-side: the customer experience without the AI advisor, vs with it.

✗  Without AI Advisor
  • Visitor lands on a grid of 100+ products — no hierarchy, no guidance
  • Product names (Reload, Recover, Activate) are evocative but not intuitive
  • Technical specs dominate — grams, BCAA ratios, serving sizes confuse beginners
  • Free nutritionist consultation only available after ₹2,000 purchase
  • No diet filter for Vegetarian/Vegan/Jain preferences on product listing
  • User guesses, buys wrong product, sees no results
  • "No effect" review posted — brand trust damaged
  • Customer either returns (cost) or silently churns (lost CLV)
  • Mobile users scroll endlessly with no intelligent search layer
  • Average time to product: 8+ minutes
✓  With AI Advisor
  • Chatbot opens with a warm greeting and a clear promise: 4 questions, 1 perfect product
  • Questions use plain language — "what's your goal?" not "what's your macronutrient target?"
  • Diet filter (Veg/Vegan/Jain) ensures recommendation is always ethically appropriate
  • Every recommendation explains WHY before naming WHAT — builds trust
  • Beginners get a single, safe starting product — no overwhelming stacks
  • Experienced users get a full pre/during/post stack with timing instructions
  • Confidence score shown — medium-confidence triggers a 2-option comparison
  • 👍/👎 feedback loop enables continuous model improvement
  • Mobile-first design — entire flow works in under 90 seconds on a phone
  • Average time to product: <3 minutes
